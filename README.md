# Novel Writing Studio

A free, open-source web application designed for novelists and creative writers. Write, organize, and track your novel‚Äîall in your browser with automatic local storage.

## ‚ú® Features

- **Chapter Management** - Organize your novel into chapters with automatic word counting
- **Character Database** - Track character details, roles, traits, and character arcs
- **Location Tracker** - Document important places in your story world
- **Plot Timeline** - Visual timeline of your story's plot points with status tracking
- **Daily Writing Goals** - Set word count goals and track your progress
- **14-Day History** - See your writing streak and productivity
- **Export to Word** - Download your complete novel as a .doc file
- **Auto-Save** - All data automatically saves to your browser's localStorage
- **Privacy-First** - Your data never leaves your device
